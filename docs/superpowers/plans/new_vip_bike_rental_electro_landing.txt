Here is the completely overhauled VIP Bike landing page. I have strictly applied the "high-end fitness studio" aesthetic to the bike rental concept—transforming it into a hardcore, professional, and community-driven experience. 

### What changed:
*   **Color Palette:** Transitioned from gold-on-black to a brutalist **Pure Black (#050505) + Dark Gray (#111)** base, with **Neon Orange (#FF4500)** and **Metallic Silver (#E0E0E0)** accents.
*   **Photography & Imagery:** All images are now forced into high-contrast black and white via CSS filters (`grayscale(100%) contrast(120%)`) with neon orange overlays, emphasizing the machinery's "muscle" and texture.
*   **Typography & Whitespace:** Removed all rounded corners (`rounded-none`), implemented condensed, bold, uppercase sans-serif typography, and packed the information tightly for a high-density, forceful layout.
*   **Structure Mapping:** 
    *   *Hero:* Brand attitude + hardcore CTA.
    *   *Curriculum (BikeShowcase):* Bikes organized by riding "goals" (Adrenaline, Cruising, Enduro).
    *   *Coach Profiles (TheCrew):* New section introducing the mechanics/instructors as certified professionals.
    *   *Member Stories:* New section featuring rider transformations.
    *   *Book a Trial:* Integrated aggressive CTAs throughout.

### Final Notes on the Aesthetic Shift:
1. **The Gritty Texture:** The SVG noise filter in `globals.css` adds an imperceptible grain over the whole site, simulating the texture of gym equipment/road asphalt.
2. **Typography is Key:** By using `Barlow Condensed` (variable font injected via `next/font`), your `<h1>` to `<h3>` tags will naturally look tall, aggressive, and condensed. Just make sure to apply `font-heading` to your headings or rely on the bold/uppercase classes already provided in the components.
3. **No Rounded Corners:** Enforcing `border-radius: 0 !important` in the CSS guarantees that brutalist, sharp-edged look that high-end fitness studios love, keeping the design uncompromisingly strict.
